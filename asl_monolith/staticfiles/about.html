About ASL Recognition

This platform utilizes advanced machine learning technology to recognize American Sign Language alphabet letters in real-time, making communication more accessible and inclusive.

Built with Django, OpenCV, MediaPipe, and scikit-learn, this system provides accurate real-time recognition of ASL hand gestures through your webcam.
